Kit Room

Kit Room shares VICE's extensive knowledge of filmmaking through the viewfinder of the LUMIX GH5 a stills camera with exceptional video capability that not only builds on the heritage of the LUMIX GH4 but blows our expectations out of the water. From its compact size, enabling us to shoot dynamic footage from a moving car, to using an XLR adaptor to record flawless sound, Kit Room explores the GH5 step by step,a camera with outstanding video specification, super-swift capture and incredible colour reproduction, a real all-round performer in a compact, durable body.

Prop Room

A prop, or property, is any moveable item that can be seen on a film. It could be a hat, gun, cushion, wine glass, lightsaber, carpet, kitchen unit, tree or aircraft. Prop masters run the property department which makes, stores and transports the props as well as preps the props for each day's shoot.

Sound Recording Suite

What is sound recording in filmmaking? What is sound recording in film? It involves capturing various sound components, including dialogue, ambient noises, sound effects, and music during filming. These sound recordings become the foundation for creating a rich and immersive auditory experience that enhances the film's storytelling and emotional impact.
